About JGC

John Gray Constructions (JGC) has been a constant and integral part of the construction industry within Townsville and surrounding North Queensland region for over a decade and has grown into a leader within the industry. JGC are focussed on promoting and growing local construction companies and ensuring the organic capacity of locally owned and operated companies can effectively compete with larger non-local companies.
